# About

The Tetraminoes Wiki is a website created by Breadlicia and RunTheBot.

## Mission

Tetraminoes purpose is to provide good quality, easily accesible, and relevant information in all aspects of Tetris.

## Contribute

Tetraminoes consists of information written by the people for the people. The articles are freely editable to almost anyone with internet access. This project relies on your contributions in order to fulfill the mission and stay sustainable, please consider contributing.

## Contact

If you would like to voice concerns, questions, or suggestion please don't hesitate to contact us. You can send us a message via Discord @Breadlicia or @RunTheBot.
